About BBB

The best sister and friend a girl can ask for.

Bisola Aiyeola is a Nigerian actress and singer. In 2017, Bisola became the first runner up of Big Brother Naija. In 2018, she won the AMVCA Trailblazer Award at the 2018 Africa Magic Viewers Choice Awards. In 2021, she was one of the investors of Amaze, an app that makes endorsement deals easier for celebrities.She was also one of the contestants at MTN Project Fame West Africa in 2008, where she finished 5th and Iyanya won. From 2011-to 2013, the fast-rising entertainer served as a TV host for Billboard Nigeria, which aired on Silverbird Television.
